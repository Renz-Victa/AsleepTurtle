Common Questions

Frequently asked

What exactly is an AI Strategy & Roadmap?

An AI Strategy & Roadmap is a structured engagement where we assess your organisation's current data maturity, team capabilities and business goals. Then we build a prioritised, actionable plan for where and how AI can create the most value. You leave with a clear picture of what to do and how to get your board and teams aligned behind it.

Do we need existing AI infrastructure?

Not at all. Many clients come with zero AI in production. Our assessment works at any stage — the output reflects where you are and the fastest path forward.

How much system access do you need?

We work through structured interviews and documentation reviews. We don't need direct database or system access — protecting your IP and security is a priority.

Can this be used for our funding round?

Yes. Many founders use the output to demonstrate a credible AI strategy to Series A/B investors. We can tailor the executive presentation to your investor audience.

What happens after the engagement ends?

We will send a follow-up check-in engagement 6–12 months later.

Is this a generic report, or is it tailored to my business?

Everything is built around your specific business - your industry, your data infrastructure, your team, your competitive landscape and your goals. The use cases we identify, the ROI estimates we build and the roadmap we deliver are specific to your company. Generic AI reports are everywhere - what we produce is a working plan your team can execute..

We already have an internal data team. Do we still need this?

Often, yes and here's why. Internal teams are excellent at execution, but are often too close to the day-to-day to step back and prioritise objectively. They also lack a benchmark for what's achievable, because they only see one company. We bring an outside view, cross-industry experience and the ability to challenge assumptions that have become internal assumptions. Most of our clients have internal data teams - we work alongside them, not instead of them.

We've tried AI before and it didn't work. Why would this be different?

Most companies fail not because the technology doesn't work, but because they start with the wrong use case, underestimate data readiness, or lack organisational buy-in. Our process begins by diagnosing exactly why previous efforts were stalled. We then design the roadmap specifically to avoid those failure modes, including quick wins in the first 90 days to build internal confidence before tackling larger initiatives.

How long does the engagement take?

A standard AI Strategy & Roadmap engagement runs 12 months from kickoff to final delivery. We keep it focused - this is not a 6-month consulting project.

What does the process actually look like?

Day 1 - discovery: stakeholder interviews across leadership, data, and operations teams. We map your current state honestly.
Day 2 - assessment: we audit your data infrastructure, tech stack, existing tools, and skill gaps against industry benchmarks.
Day 3 - use case development: we identify and score 5–15 AI use cases by ROI potential, feasibility, and strategic fit.
Day 4–5 - roadmap and delivery: we build the phased roadmap, governance framework, and executive presentation, then present findings to your leadership team.

How much of our team's time will this require?

We design the engagement to be lightweight on your side. Typically we need 3–4 hours from your key stakeholders across the full engagement — mostly in structured interviews and one final presentation session. We do the heavy lifting.
